Meet Rusty! Red pandas prefer to stick very close to their home range, but the Smithsonian National Zoo posted this pick when Rusty went missing... As a community, where are librarians currently at? Throughout the last century, librarians have adapted their methods and practices to make the best possible use of new technologies, be it the standardization of card catalogs, databases or full-text indexing. However, the arrival of the Web now more than two decades ago has fundamentally shifted how society at large creates, shares and searches for information.
