Meet Rusty, the red panda! These animals prefer to stick very close to their home range, but the Smithsonian National Zoo posted this picture when Rusty went missing... As a community, where are we at as librarians currently at? Throughout the 20th century, librarians have embedded new technologies within their profession. The introduction of Linked Data within the library community offers enormous potential for sharing information in a more structured manner on the Web. However, some of the conceptual and operational aspects of Linked Data seem exotic or even outright scandalous to a librarian. These ten modules will allow you to better understand the possibilities and challenges

Are Linked Data as Nile perch? This fish was artificially introduced in lakes across the African continent for economical reasons. However, this fish eats up all other fishes and completely wrecks the ecological balance. Are Linked Data colonizing and corrupting library metadata ?

Or can library metadata and Linked Data peacefully co-exist and leverage one another ? These tend modules will help you develop a more nuanced perspective on Linked Data !
